Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, then follow the reset link sent to your email.
---
Q: How do I track my order?
A: Go to "My Orders" → select the order → click "Track". If tracking is unavailable, contact support with your order ID.
---
Q: My payment failed, what should I do?
A: Check your card balance, ensure OTP or 3D-secure is completed, then retry. If money was deducted but order not confirmed, contact support with payment reference.
---
Q: How do I request a refund?
A: Go to Orders → Select the order → Click "Request Refund" and choose the reason. Refunds take 5–7 business days after approval.
---
Q: Where can I download the invoice?
A: Go to Orders → Select the order → Click "Download Invoice".
